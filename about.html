About Dinghy Lab

Dinghy Lab is a free collection of sailing tools built around the World Sailing Racing Rules of Sailing 2025-2028: an interactive right-of-way simulator and a two-player dinghy race game on one keyboard. It was created for university sailing club beginners and is available in English, Korean, Spanish, French and German.

Who it's for

  • New sailing club members learning port/starboard and windward/leeward for the first time
  • Beginner racers who want to rehearse right-of-way calls before getting on the water
  • Anyone who wants to feel a start sequence, mark roundings and penalties in a game

Note

Both tools simplify the rules for learning. In real racing, the current RRS text, sailing instructions and race officials always take precedence.
