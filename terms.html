Educational simplification

The simulator and the race game present simplified versions of the World Sailing Racing Rules of Sailing 2025-2028 (basic right of way, Rules 10-13; related limitations, Rules 14-17; mark-room overview, Rule 18; game penalties inspired by Rules 28, 31 and 44). They are not complete race-judging tools.

Out of scope

  • Complete mark-room decisions (zone-entry timing and overlap history are required)
  • Obstructions and room to tack (Rules 19-20), starting-line special cases, team racing appendices
  • Exoneration limits when damage, injury or significant advantage occurs

Limitation of liability

The operator accepts no liability for any damage arising from decisions based on this site's output. In real sailing, always follow the current RRS text, notices of race, sailing instructions, coaches and race officials — and put collision avoidance first.

Copyright

The Racing Rules of Sailing are copyright World Sailing. This site does not reproduce the rule text; it only provides simplified educational summaries.
