PRIVACY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

1. Information we collect

This site has no accounts, comments, contact forms or server database. We do not directly collect personal information such as names, emails or phone numbers.

2. Local browser storage

Your language choice, quiz best score and consent preference are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device and is never sent to the site operator. Site files may be cached by your browser (Service Worker) for offline use.

3. Advertising (Google AdSense) and analytics

With your consent, this site may serve Google AdSense ads and use Google Analytics with anonymized IP. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google may use advertising cookies to provide personalized ads.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • How Google uses data from partner sites: Google policy page.
  • You can change your consent anytime via the "Privacy settings" link in the footer.

4. External links

The site links to external pages such as the World Sailing rules page. Their own privacy policies apply there.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the site operator.

개인정보처리방침 요약 (한국어)

이 사이트는 개인정보를 직접 수집하지 않습니다. 언어 설정·퀴즈 기록·동의 여부는 사용자의 기기(localStorage)에만 저장됩니다. 동의한 경우에 한해 Google AdSense 광고와 익명화된 Google Analytics가 사용될 수 있으며, Google을 포함한 제3자는 쿠키를 사용해 이전 방문 기록 기반의 광고를 게재할 수 있습니다. 맞춤 광고는 Google 광고 설정에서 해제할 수 있고, 푸터의 "개인정보 설정"에서 언제든 동의를 변경할 수 있습니다.
